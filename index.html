Just A Basic Bitch

You think I'm joking? Clearly not. You must have hit your head on the way here or ate something bad that you lost your freaking mind. With that said, I am starting to doubt whether or not wanting to know you.

Living with passion and no regrets is what I strictly adhere to. "Always do what you want before you die" how much hackneyed this sounds it holds the absolute truth. That is why I love travelling. Not for the sole reason of seeing the world outside home, but also helps me to escape reality and venture into a different dimensions of my own solitude. As a travel freak of myself, I love to experience different kind of cultures alongside with the people I lived to tell the tales.

I am a continous learner and also a passion storyteller. Learning for me equates to exploring new ideas and growing as an individual. I just cannot imagine my life devoid of designing. I freely express my creativity and imagination by designing with a little bit spice of storytelling to satisfy my curiosity of the world has to offer.
